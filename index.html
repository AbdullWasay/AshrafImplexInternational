About Ashraf Impex International

With over 25 years of excellence in the leather industry, Ashraf Impex International stands as a trusted bridge between premium leather suppliers in Pakistan and markets globally, with a special focus on Europe. Our deep understanding of both local craftsmanship and international quality standards has made us a preferred partner for businesses seeking reliable, sustainable, and premium leather solutions. We pride ourselves on building long-term relationships based on trust, quality, and environmental responsibility.

Ashraf Impex International

With 25 years of experience in the leather industry, we are a premier buying and selling leather agency specializing in quality control and inspection. We work with 20+ partner tanneries in Pakistan, all LWG certified, and export to 10+ countries worldwide. We act as trusted agents, meticulously checking leather on behalf of our clients to ensure the highest standards are met.

Sister Company - Ashraf Leather Pvt. Ltd.

Our sister company is a dedicated exporter of leather specializing in upper and lining leather (finished, semi-dyed, and crust) in cow, buffalo, goat, and hair sheep varieties. We also manufacture leather products according to buyers' specific demands and requirements.

Our Heritage

Based in Karachi, Pakistan, we have built our reputation on quality, reliability, and sustainable practices. Our focus on LWG-certified tanneries ensures that every piece of leather meets the highest international standards.

Sustainable Practices

At Ashraf Impex International, we proudly maintain the highest standards of environmental stewardship throughout our operations:

Certified Partnerships

We exclusively source finished leather from tanneries certified by the Leather Working Group (LWG), ensuring responsible production and environmental compliance.

Advanced Environmental Practices

Our partner tanneries utilize eco‑efficient water and chemical management systems, along with chrome‑free tanning options and low‑impact dyes.

Traceability & Waste Management

From hide to finished leather, every step is traceable and managed to minimize waste—backed by stringent audits, full transparency, and proper by‑product reuse.

Continuous Improvement

We support ongoing LWG-led initiatives aimed at reducing environmental impact, from deforestation due diligence to advanced chemical controls.
