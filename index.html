About Ashraf Impex International

With over 25 years of excellence in the leather industry, Ashraf Impex International stands as a trusted bridge between premium leather suppliers and discerning European markets.

Our Heritage

Based in Karachi, Pakistan, we have built our reputation on quality, reliability, and sustainable practices. Our focus on LWG-certified tanneries ensures that every piece of leather meets the highest international standards.

Sister Company

Together with our sister company Ashraf Leather Pvt. Ltd., we offer comprehensive leather solutions from sourcing to final delivery.
